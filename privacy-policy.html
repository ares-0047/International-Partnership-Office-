Privacy Policy & Compliance

Quantum University International Partnership Office (IPO) is committed to protecting the privacy of our global delegates, students, and faculty in strict accordance with national and international regulatory frameworks.

gavel 1. Regulatory Adherence (UGC & MEA)

All international collaborations, student exchanges, and faculty mobility programs facilitated by the IPO strictly adhere to the guidelines set forth by the University Grants Commission (UGC) and the Ministry of External Affairs (MEA), Government of India.

Data collected for visa processing, diplomatic clearances, and international academic registry is processed solely for the fulfillment of statutory obligations and institutional compliance.

handshake 2. Cross-Border Data Transfer under MoUs

Information shared with foreign partner universities is governed by the specific confidentiality clauses embedded within our formally executed Memorandums of Understanding (MoUs). Data transferred across borders is limited to what is strictly necessary for academic integration, credit transfer, and logistical support.

  • Partner institutions are bound by mutual non-disclosure agreements (NDAs).
  • Student academic records are encrypted during international digital transmission.
  • No commercial monetization of student or faculty profiles is permitted under any MoU.

database 3. Data Collection and Usage

We collect personally identifiable information (PII) such as passport details, academic transcripts, and institutional affiliations through secure channels like our Diplomatic Inquiries portal. This data is utilized exclusively for:

  • Evaluating partnership proposals and drafting official agreements.
  • Facilitating international student and faculty mobility programs.
  • Issuing official Letters of Invitation for visa applications.
